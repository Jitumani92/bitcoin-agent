Bitcoin metrics line up bull signals with $78K the BTC price level to beat

📰 Cointelegraph.com News 📅 April 21, 2026 Original Source →

In a potential sign of an upcoming bull run, two classic technical indicators are pointing towards a breakout in Bitcoin's price. The first indicator is the relative strength index (RSI), which measures the speed and change of price movements, suggesting that Bitcoin may have bottomed out at around $75,000. If this holds true, it could mean that Bitcoin has found a new floor and is primed for another surge in value.

The second indicator is the Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD), which tracks the relationship between two moving averages of a security's price. A bullish cross, where the MACD line crosses above the signal line, is often seen as a sign of an upcoming price increase. In Bitcoin's case, this cross has occurred and points towards a potential breakout above the $78,000 level.

These bullish signals come at a time when many investors are watching Bitcoin closely for signs of recovery after its steep drop in value earlier this year. If these indicators hold true and Bitcoin does indeed break through the $78,000 level, it could mark the beginning of a new bull market for the world's largest cryptocurrency.

💡 Key Takeaway

The convergence of two classic technical indicators suggests that Bitcoin may be gearing up for another price surge, with potential for a breakout above $78,000.
