Bitcoin metrics line up bull signals with $78K the BTC price level to beat
📰 Cointelegraph.com News 📅 April 21, 2026
In a potential sign of an upcoming bull run, two classic technical indicators are pointing towards a breakout in Bitcoin's price. The second indicator is the Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD), which tracks the relationship between two moving averages of a security's price. A bullish cross, where the MACD line crosses above the signal line, is often seen as a sign of an upcoming price increase. In Bitcoin's case, this cross has occurred and points towards a potential breakout above the $78,000 level.
